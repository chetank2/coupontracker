Coupon Tracker

Coupon Tracker helps you save coupon screenshots, organize offer details, and get expiry reminders without losing codes in your gallery or chats.

A simple place to learn what the app does and how it handles privacy.

Privacy Policy

App Overview

Use the camera or upload a screenshot, review the extracted coupon details, and save offers in one place. The app is designed around on-device processing after model setup.

  • Save coupon screenshots and offer codes
  • Review extracted store, description, and expiry details
  • Manage multiple detected coupons from a single image
  • Get reminder notifications before coupons expire
