## App name
Coupon Tracker

## Short description
Save coupon screenshots, review extracted details, and get expiry reminders.

## Full description
Coupon Tracker helps you save discount coupons from screenshots, promo images, and shared coupon links without turning your inbox or gallery into a mess.

Use the camera or upload a screenshot, let the on-device extractor read visible coupon text, then review and save the result. Coupon Tracker keeps your saved offers in one place, highlights expiring coupons, and reminds you before they go stale.

Key capabilities:
- Scan or upload coupon screenshots
- Extract draft coupon details with on-device processing
- Save store, description, code, and expiry date
- Review multiple detected coupons before saving
- Set reminder notifications for expiring offers
- Export and restore encrypted backups

Transparency:
- Coupon Tracker is a personal organization and reminder tool, not a financial service, payment service, gambling app, lending app, or investment app
- OCR and on-device extraction can be incomplete or wrong; users should review coupon details before saving or redeeming
- The app does not guarantee that a coupon is valid, accepted by a merchant, or will produce savings
- The app is not affiliated with stores, brands, or coupon issuers shown in user screenshots unless explicitly stated

Privacy:
- Coupon extraction is designed to run on device after model setup
- Coupon images are not uploaded as part of normal offline extraction
- Model downloads happen only when you request them
