Coupon Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-13

Coupon Tracker is designed to process coupon screenshots and coupon metadata primarily on your device.

What the app stores
- Coupon details that you save, such as store name, offer description, coupon code, redeem code, expiry date, reminder state, and optional notes.
- Local app preferences, such as theme, reminder settings, onboarding completion, and model download state.
- Optional local logs or diagnostics that remain on device unless you export them yourself.

What the app accesses
- Camera, when you choose to scan a coupon.
- Photos or files, when you choose to import an image or restore a backup.
- Notifications, when you enable coupon expiry reminders.
- Network, only when you choose to download an on-device model file or import supported remote assets. Coupon extraction itself is intended to run offline after model setup.

How data is used
- To extract coupon information from screenshots you provide.
- To save coupons locally and remind you before expiry.
- To download model files you explicitly request.

Transparency and limitations
- Coupon Tracker is a personal organization and reminder tool. It is not a financial service, payment service, gambling app, lending app, or investment app.
- Coupon extraction uses OCR, rules, and optional on-device models. Results can be incomplete or wrong, so you should review coupon details before saving or redeeming them.
- Coupon Tracker does not guarantee that a coupon is valid, redeemable, accepted by a merchant, or that it will produce any savings.
- Coupon Tracker is not affiliated with the stores, brands, or coupon issuers that may appear in screenshots unless explicitly stated elsewhere.

How data is shared
- Coupon Tracker does not send your coupon images or coupon contents to our servers as part of normal on-device extraction.
- If you use export or backup features, data is only shared to the destination you choose.
- If you choose to download a model, the app contacts approved model-hosting endpoints to fetch model files.

Security
- Network downloads are restricted to approved hosts and HTTPS.
- Backup/export flows are intended to use encrypted formats where supported by the app configuration.

Data retention and deletion
- Your coupon data stays on your device until you delete individual coupons, clear app data, uninstall the app, or overwrite a restore target.
- Downloaded model files remain on device until you delete them from the app settings or uninstall the app.

Children
- Coupon Tracker is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
